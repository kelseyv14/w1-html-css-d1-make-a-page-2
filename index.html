whaaaaat:

Today's consumer is not scouring editorial fashion magazines for what is 'in and out'.
There are an estimated 52 micro-seasons a year that leave buyers over-inundated
with trends that are far diluted from the ethically prepared garments innovators and artists
are creating in what is known (among other titles) as the process of slow fashion.
By making a concious effort to purchase clothing made with sustainable fabrics and textiles,
thrifting for secondhand clothes, and incorperating vintage pieces into your wardrobe, you are
decreasing your carbon footprint & effectively making the world more beautiful by creating less waste!
